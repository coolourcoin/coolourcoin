CoolourCoin — Whitepaper (HTML)

Light‑backed, floor‑protected digital colours.

Abstract

Most crypto assets are volatile and hard to understand. CoolourCoin proposes a colour‑first economy: finite shades of light, each protected by an immutable Spectral Floor — the highest price ever achieved by that colour.

1. Palette & scarcity

Genesis: 4,096 shades. Each token represents a unique RGB triplet; tiers reflect rarity towards white.

2. Mining protocol

RGB team mining synchronizes three people to commit the exact value of a newly released shade. Licenses allocate per‑channel capacity; value used sets the initial floor proportionally.

3. Spectral Floor rule

On‑chain enforcement prevents any sale below the recorded floor. OTC escapes are handled by official escrow that refuses transfers under floor.

4. Market design

Direct purchase at tier price, secondary marketplace with floor enforcement, split/merge mechanics and Assemblies with fixed creation fee.

5. Governance of trust

No passive yields or staking. Open contracts, public audits, and legal compliance. Clarity over hype.
