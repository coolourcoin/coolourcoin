Abstract

Most crypto assets are volatile and hard to reason about. CoolourCoin proposes value that honours cost: every colour is a unique on‑chain asset with a non‑decreasing price floor equal to its highest purchase price.

Mechanics

  • Team mining (RGB) with license capacity.
  • Protocol direct purchase.
  • Spectral Floor enforced on‑chain (no admin override).
  • Divisible by thresholds; mergeable by full ownership.
  • Open treasury, transparent fees.
