CoolourCoin

Light‑backed, floor‑protected digital colours. This draft outlines the Genesis palette, Spectral Floor Rule, RGB mining, assemblies, and the treasury model.

1. Vision

CoolourCoin transforms finite shades of light into verifiable, rare digital assets. It is simple enough for non‑crypto people, fair enough for communities, and transparent enough for investors.

2. Palette & scarcity

Genesis ≈4096 curated colours in tiers. Higher resolution spectra unlock as adoption grows. Each colour has deterministic rarity derived from distance to white and perceptual metrics.

3. Spectral Floor Rule

For each colour id, a non‑decreasing floor is enforced at marketplace level. Once a colour sells at price P, no future sale can list below P.

4. RGB Team Mining

Three users combine exact triplets (R,G,B). When a window opens for a triplet, the online matching team mints it. License units are deducted proportionally.

5. Assemblies & splits

Colours can be assembled into verified sets (e.g., flags) via a one‑time fee; splits and merges are deterministic and on‑chain, preserving floors and provenance.

6. Treasury & fees

Transparent fee split: protocol ops, reserve, public audit. No passive yield; no leverage. Floor protection avoids panic‑dump dynamics.

7. Security

Open‑source contracts, timelock upgrades, per‑team limits, verifiable randomness for colour windows, and audit‑ready event logs.
