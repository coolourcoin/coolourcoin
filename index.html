How it works Genesis spectrum (4096) Read whitepaper

1) Spectral Floor (never below)

Each colour has a permanent price floor equal to its all‑time high sale. Listed anywhere below? Blocked at the protocol level.

2) Two ways to obtain

Mine as an RGB team using licences, or Buy Direct from the protocol at tier‑based prices. No lotteries. No hype.

3) RGB social mining

Three humans, three channels (R, G, B). Commit the exact shade during an open window. If one drops, a 15‑minute grace; after that, the window reopens.

4) Licences

Simple, capped, and time‑released. Early are cheaper; later rarer → pricier. Licences fuel mining and anchor initial value.

5) Splits & Merges

Split a colour into on‑chain fractions when price milestones are met. Merge back by reuniting all parts. Floor always follows the max sale.

6) Assemblies

Combine multiple colours (e.g., a national flag). A fixed fee (to treasury) sets a floor for the assembly. Dissolve later if you own them all.

7) Treasury

Holds colours and fees to stabilise markets, fund audits, and pay contributors with on‑chain assets, not promises.

8) Open, auditable

Core contracts are public. Randomness = verifiable (onion RNG). No admin backdoors for price floors. No passive yield.

9) Human‑first

CoolourCoin is not a casino. It’s a resting place for light — and a tool to store value without dumps.
