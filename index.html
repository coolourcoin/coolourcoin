The four pillars of trust

Spectral Floor

Each colour keeps the highest price it has ever reached on the official market. The protocol never allows a sale below that floor.

Finite Genesis Palette

CoolourCoin starts from a finite set of genesis colours. No unbounded inflation, no endless minting – just a clean, auditable spectrum.

RGB Social Mining

Colours are mined by small RGB teams. Three people, three channels (R, G, B) working together to unlock a single shade.

No yield, no leverage

There is no staking, no passive yield and no leverage in the protocol. The only guarantee: a colour cannot be pushed below its own light.

A declaration of value

CoolourCoin is not a casino. It is a resting place for light.

When you hold a colour, you hold a right. When you sell it, you honour the floor. When you mine, you become part of the spectrum.
