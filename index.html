The trust geometry of CoolourCoin

1. Finite Genesis Spectrum

CoolourCoin starts from a fixed, public Genesis Spectrum of 4,096 base colours. No hidden mint. No secret supply.

2. Spectral Floor Rule

Each colour stores the highest price it has ever been bought for. The protocol enforces: no sale below that floor.

3. Human-coordinated mining

A colour can only be mined by a live RGB team: three people, three licences, three channels (R, G, B) online at the same time.

4. Licence-based entry

Access to mining is done through time-limited licences for R, G and B. No GPUs, no hardware race — just presence, coordination and spectrum.

5. Direct colour purchase

Some colours can be acquired directly from the protocol at a deterministic price, always above the mining cost. This keeps miners and buyers in balance.

6. Transparent treasury

A portion of licence revenue feeds the Treasury. From there, colours can be used to pay contributors or form long-term reserves.

7. No yield, no leverage

CoolourCoin does not pay interest, does not auto-compound and does not promise any financial return. It only protects the floor.

8. Open-source & verifiable

The contract logic is open-source, audit-ready and focused on one core guarantee: you cannot push a colour below its own history.
