How it works — in 30 seconds

1

Get licences

Each channel (R, G, B) has a simple fixed price at start. Licences hold capacity.

2

Form a team

Three people connect as R, G or B and choose a target shade (e.g., R200 G128 B099).

3

Mine the exact shade

When the protocol opens a window for that shade, the online team that matches it mints the colour.

4

Spectral floor

The colour’s minimum price is set to the highest price ever paid. Listings below floor are rejected.
