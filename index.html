Spectral Floor

On‑chain rule: a colour can’t be sold below its highest purchase price. Trust via logic, not promises.

No backdoors. No admin overrides.

RGB Team Mining

Three people, three channels (R, G, B). When your exact combination matches the released shade, you mint it. Simple, social, fair.

Presence‑based mining, not hardware wars.

Direct Purchase

Prefer to buy? Choose a colour directly from the active tier at a protocol price, with the same floor guarantee.

Anti‑dump by design.

Open Treasury

Licenses, fees and protocol allocations flow into a public treasury used for maintenance, audits and bounties.

Transparent by default.

Divisible, Not Dilutable

High‑value colours can split into smaller units with independent floors — or merge back when a holder owns them all.

Stability > speculation.

One‑Click Clarity

Everything is readable: transaction explorer, colour provenance, team mining logs, and license history.
