Genesis Spectrum

The Genesis Spectrum is the first and only full layer of CoolourCoin: 4,096 unique base colours, arranged as a spectral pyramid.

Each tile in the pyramid is a potential asset. It will be mined once, acquire a floor once, and then live under the rule of its own history.

Genesis Spectrum — 4,096 colours. Finite, public, non-replicable.

Tiers and rarity

Colours closer to white form the upper tiers of the pyramid. They are fewer, brighter, and naturally tend to attract higher bids. The lower tiers hold broader, deeper shades — more accessible, but still unique.

The mapping (Tier, ID, RGB, HEX, rarity) is defined once and will be published as an open dataset. The protocol does not hide supply.

Origin is recorded forever

When a team mines a Genesis colour, the protocol records:

  • the colour ID and RGB code,
  • the initial floor, based on licence capacity used,
  • the discoverer IDs (team members).

Ownership may change many times after that. The origin does not.
