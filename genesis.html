Genesis Spectrum

The Genesis Spectrum is the foundation of CoolourCoin: 4,096 unique base colours, arranged as a spectral pyramid.

Each tile is a potential asset. It will be mined once, assigned a floor once, and live under that rule for as long as the protocol exists.

4,096 colours. Finite, public, non-replicable.

Tiers of rarity

Colours closer to white form the upper tiers — fewer, brighter, and naturally more contested. Deeper shades form the lower tiers — broader, richer, but still strictly unique.

The full mapping (ID, RGB, HEX, Tier) will be published as an open dataset, ready for independent verification, analysis and art.

Origin and memory

Once a team mines a colour, the protocol records:

  • the colour ID and its exact RGB code,
  • the initial spectral floor,
  • the discoverer IDs.

Ownership can change. The origin does not. The first discovery is part of the colour’s permanent story.
