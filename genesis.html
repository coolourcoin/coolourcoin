Genesis Spectrum

The finite, carefully arranged palette of 4 096 base colours from which the entire CoolourCoin universe is born.

The Genesis Spectrum is organised as a spectral pyramid: wide and rich at the base with deep colours, narrowing towards the apex where the rarest near-white shades live.

Every block in this pyramid is:

  • a unique coordinate in RGB space,
  • bound to one and only one CoolourCoin asset,
  • tagged with its rarity tier and spectral neighbours.

Once a colour is mined or bought, that coordinate is taken forever. The spectrum does not inflate. It only reveals itself, line by line.

“You cannot re-mint a sunrise. You can only remember the light.”
