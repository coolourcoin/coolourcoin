Genesis Spectrum

A finite, ordered set of colours – the starting point for all CoolourCoin assets.

The 4,096-colour core

At the foundation of CoolourCoin sits a finite genesis palette, composed of 4,096 carefully quantised colours. Each block in the spectrum represents a single, unique shade that can be mined, owned and transferred – but never duplicated.

The palette is structured: darker, more saturated tones near the base; softer mid-tones in the centre; rare near-white shades towards the apex. This creates a natural hierarchy of scarcity without artificial tricks.

You cannot re-mint a sunrise.
You can only remember the light.

Once a genesis colour leaves the spectrum and is mined into existence, it carries with it an immutable record: who first discovered it, what mining costs were paid, and which path its price took over time.
