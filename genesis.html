Genesis Spectrum

4,096 colours • finite • luminous • tiered

Origin of light

1. Why 4,096 colours?

CoolourCoin starts from a controlled, finite palette. 4,096 is big enough to feel like a real spectrum, but small enough to keep rarity clear. Every colour in this genesis set is precomputed, ordered and identifiable.

This gives us a stable “map of light” — we know what exists, what was mined, and what is still locked.

Uniqueness

2. One colour, one existence

Each colour in the Genesis Spectrum can be minted only once. After it is mined or sold, it is marked as claimed. Other users can only trade it, never recreate it.

Nearby shades can appear later in extended spectrums, but the genesis one is special — it’s the “first light” of CoolourCoin.

The light pyramid

3. Tiered rarity

The 4,096 colours are arranged in a pyramid of rarity, based on distance from pure white (#FFFFFF):

Tier 0 — White / Near-White

Ultra rare. May be auctioned or reserved in Treasury. Appears rarely, like aurora bursts.

Tier 1 — Primaries of Light

Key RGB-like tones, used for symbolic and brand colours.

Tier 2 — Derived & pastel spectrum

Where most artistic / national flag colours will live.

Tier 3 / 4 — Broad base

Accessible, mineable, good for community onboarding.

Discovery

4. Found once, remembered forever

When a team of 3 mines a genesis colour, the platform saves:

  • colour ID (from the 4,096)
  • exact RGB/HEX
  • timestamp
  • the 3 discoverer wallets / usernames

This entry becomes part of the public history of CoolourCoin. Even if the colour is sold 100 times later, the first team stays written.

Future layers

5. Above Genesis

Spectrum can later be expanded into much finer palettes (full 24-bit colour = 16,777,216 shades), but only after Genesis is mostly mined and the community votes for expansion.

This keeps speculation healthy and prevents dilution. New layers can have different rules (e.g. artists-only, brand-only, event-only).
