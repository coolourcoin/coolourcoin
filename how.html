How CoolourCoin works

Simple, transparent, human. The first light‑backed asset with a hard Spectral Floor.

1) Palette & tiers

We begin with a finite Genesis palette (4,096 shades) arranged from dark to white. Each colour has an ID, an RGB/HEX code, a tier and rarity rank.

2) Mining with teams (RGB)

  • Create a team of three. One plays Red, one Green, one Blue.
  • When a colour appears (e.g., R200 G128 B099), a team holding the exact values commits it first and wins.
  • Licenses allocate capacity per channel (R/G/B) and deplete proportionally to what you used.
  • One team can mine only one colour at a time. Disconnect? You have 15 minutes to rejoin or the slot reopens.

3) Spectral Floor (price protection)

Every colour stores the highest price it ever achieved. Marketplace enforces never below floor, on‑chain.

4) Direct purchase

Any available colour can be bought directly at its tier price. This sets the initial floor. High‑tier catalogue prices are intentionally steep to protect miners’ upside.

5) Splits & merges

  • Split: pricey colours can be split into smaller, tradable parts once thresholds are met.
  • Merge: you can recombine siblings into the parent token if you hold them all.
  • Provenance, history, and floors are preserved across operations.

6) Assemblies (e.g., flags, logos)

Own multiple colours that form a meaningful set? Lock them into an Assembly. A fixed fee mints the bundle with its own identity and floor. Dissolving returns the colours; the fee is not refunded.

7) Fees & treasury

Fees are minimal and public: primary sales, marketplace trades, assembly creation, and withdrawals. A portion goes to maintenance; a portion strengthens community tools. No secret taxes. No dangling yields.

8) Clarity by design

  • No passive yield. No staking. No “number go up” promises.
  • Open‑source contracts. Third‑party audits. Public changelog.
  • Compliance first: KYC/AML where law requires. Honest geofencing where we must.
