How CoolourCoin Works

The colour-based digital asset that never sells below its last price.

STEP 1

Every coin = a unique colour

CoolourCoin doesn’t sell random codes — it mints colours from a finite spectrum (Genesis 4,096). Every colour has its own ID and can exist only once. Similar shades may appear, but never the exact same one.

When a colour is discovered, the 3 users who unlocked it are saved as its discoverers forever.

STEP 2

You can’t sell cheaper than last time

When a colour is first bought or mined, its price becomes the Spectral Floor. Any future sale inside the CoolourCoin ecosystem must be at or above that value. That’s why we say: “let your value rest in light.”

✔ No undercutting
✔ No dumping
✔ Value only goes sideways or up
STEP 3

Getting a colour (3 ways)

1. Buy an available colour from the platform / treasury.

2. Mine a colour with 2 friends — you form an RGB team (one Red, one Green, one Blue).

3. Receive a colour as payment, gift or reward.

All three paths create traceable and non-devaluating ownership.

STEP 4

RGB social mining

Colours don’t appear always. The system opens “windows” where a specific colour is available — e.g. R200 G128 B099.

To claim it, 3 different users must be online at the same time, each with licences that match those values. First valid team wins. Their names are written on that colour forever.

Mining is human, not hardware.

STEP 5

Licences are the entry tickets

To mine, you buy a small licence for Red / Green / Blue. Licences have a clear price. Because you pay for them, the colour you discover inherits that value and can’t be sold below it.

Licence prices can go up over time as fewer colours remain — so early users mine cheaper.

STEP 6

Treasury keeps the ecosystem alive

A public treasury holds some rare colours (near-white, aurora tones). We use those to pay collaborators, influencers or dev work — without printing infinite tokens.

Any treasury colour can be converted into licences at the current rate, so it always has real utility.

STEP 7

Why it’s safe for non-crypto people

• you always see the colour you own
• it can’t be listed cheaper after you bought it
• you don’t need trading skills
• you can just hold it as a protected digital asset

CoolourCoin offers no passive yield, no staking and no airdrops. Its promise is simple: your colour will not be sold below what it was worth.
