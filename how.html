Mining & Trading in three clear steps

We keep it simple. Three people, one triplet, one rule that protects everyone.

1) Form an RGB team

Each teammate chooses a channel: RGB. Buy a mining license per channel. Your license holds up to 255 units; when you mine a colour R,G,B, the used units are deducted and the rest stays for later.

Example: You target R=100, G=30, B=200. If the window opens on that exact triplet while you’re online, your team mints it.

2) Spectral Floor Rule

From the first sale, a colour’s floor equals the highest price it has ever sold for. Marketplace contracts reject any listing below that floor.

3) Trade, assemble, gift

Sell a colour (never under floor), assemble sets (e.g., country flags) with a one‑time fee, or gift colours. All actions are on‑chain and auditable.

Anti‑fraud basics

  • Verifiable randomness for colour windows (no insiders, no favourites).
  • Rate limits & per‑team constraints to block bot swarms.
  • No admin backdoors; upgrades via timelock + community signal.

Legal posture

  • No passive yield, staking or promises of profit.
  • Colours are digital collectibles with transparent rules.
  • KYC/AML only on the fiat on‑ramp (when required by partners).
