How CoolourCoin works

A simple, transparent protocol that turns colours into protected digital assets.

1. The Genesis Palette

The system starts from a finite set of genesis colours (e.g. 4,096 shades) arranged in a structured spectrum. Each colour has:

  • a unique ID,
  • a precise RGB / HEX code,
  • a tier in the spectrum (from darker to near-white).

No new genesis colours can be created. The spectrum is finite, auditable and public.

2. RGB Social Mining

Mining in CoolourCoin is social, not hardware-based. To mine a colour, three people form an RGB team:

  • one holds the red licence,
  • one holds the green licence,
  • one holds the blue licence.

When the protocol opens a mining window for a target colour (for example R=120, G=40, B=200), the team must be online together and have at least those channel values available in their licences.

3. Licences and colour value

Each licence has a cost in real currency and a maximum channel capacity (for example 255 units of R, G or B). When a team successfully mines a colour:

  • a part of each licence is “spent” to reach the target RGB values;
  • the proportional cost of those used units becomes the colour’s initial price floor;
  • the remaining unused units in the licences stay with the miners, who can join future mining rounds.

The colour is then minted as a unique on-chain asset with a clearly defined minimum value.

4. Spectral Floor – price protection

The core rule of the protocol is the Spectral Floor: a colour cannot be sold below the highest price it has ever reached on the official marketplace.

The floor is enforced in the smart contract. There are no admin backdoors to lower it. Once the light of a colour has been paid for, that level can never be undercut by any later sale.

5. Buying colours directly

Not everyone will want to mine. Some people or organisations will prefer to buy colours directly:

  • lower-tier colours (darker, more common) can be priced close to the expected mining cost;
  • higher-tier colours (rare, near-white) can have premium fixed prices or auctions;
  • all purchases immediately set or raise the Spectral Floor for that colour.

Buying directly is like claiming a specific patch of the spectrum without joining mining rounds.

6. No yield, no leverage

CoolourCoin does not offer staking, passive yield, leverage or “interest-bearing” products at the protocol level. It is intentionally conservative:

  • no “APY” promises,
  • no algorithmic yield systems,
  • no built-in leverage.

The focus is on value preservation and transparent ownership, not on speculative reward engines.
