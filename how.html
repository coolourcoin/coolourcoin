How it works

Ultra‑simple and transparent. No jargon, just rules you can verify.

1

Licences

Buy capacity for R, G or B. At start, each channel has a fixed price. Capacity is consumed proportionally when you mine.

2

Teams

Three unique people (different devices/locations) form a team: one R, one G, one B. Only one colour can be mined per team at a time.

3

Mining

Pick an exact target shade. When the oscillator opens a window for that shade, if your team is online with matching capacities, you mint it.

4

Floor

The colour’s minimum listing price is the highest price ever paid for it. Smart contracts reject lower listings automatically.

5

Treasury

Marketplace fees flow to the on‑chain treasury. Auditable. No hidden minting, no magic yield.

6

Assemblies

Optionally combine colours you own (e.g., a national flag palette). Dissolve only if you own all components.

What CoolourCoin is not

  • Not a bank and not an investment product.
  • No staking, no APY, no passive yield.
  • No admin backdoors to override the floor rule.
