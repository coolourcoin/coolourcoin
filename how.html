How it works

Simple for everyone. Colours are assets; floors prevent dumping; mining needs people.

1) Spectral Floor

No sale below the highest price ever paid. Contract‑enforced.

2) RGB Team mining

Three miners (R,G,B) lock licences to claim the exact colour that appears.

3) Direct Buy

Buy a colour without mining at a tier‑based price. This sets its floor.

4) Open & safe

Open‑source contracts, audits, multisig treasury.

Onboarding (non‑crypto)

  1. Create a wallet.
  2. Get a little USDC for gas/fees.
  3. Join an RGB team (mining) or choose Direct Buy.
  4. Keep your colour or list it ≥ floor.

We never DM for payments. Only use the official contract.
